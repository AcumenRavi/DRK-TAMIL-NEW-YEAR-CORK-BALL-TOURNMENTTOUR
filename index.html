DRK CORK BALL TOURNMENT 2025

Welcome to the DRK CORK Ball Cricket Tournament! Join us for a 10-over knock-out competition with exciting prizes!

Tournament Details

  • Umpire Decision is Final
  • Match Format: 10 overs per team
  • Powerplay: 2 overs at the start, second powerplay batting team can be chosen after the 7th over
  • Ball: Vickey Cork Ball
  • Organizing Team: Not participating, directly in the final
  • Entry Fee: ₹2000 per team
  • Prizes:
    • 1st Prize: ₹7000 note: consecutive 3 win you will get the prize(WWW)
    • 2nd Prize: ₹3000 note: consecutive 2 win you will get the prize(WWL)
    • 3rd Prize: ₹2000 note: WLW you will get the prize
  • Third Place Match: 3-over match with all 3 overs as powerplay
  • Organizing Team: Will not provide the match ball
