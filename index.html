Welcome to the Village Cricket Tournament! Join us for a 10-over knock-out competition with exciting prizes!

Tournament Details

  • Umpire Decision is Final
  • Match Format: 10 overs per team
  • Powerplay: 2 overs at the start, second powerplay can be chosen after the 7th over
  • Ball: Vickey Cork Ball
  • Organizing Team: Not participating, directly in the final
  • Entry Fee: ₹2000 per team
  • Prizes:
    • 1st Prize: ₹7000
    • 2nd Prize: ₹3000
    • 3rd Prize: ₹2000 (for 3rd place match)
  • Third Place Match: 3-over match with all 3 overs as powerplay
  • Organizing Team: Will not provide the match ball
